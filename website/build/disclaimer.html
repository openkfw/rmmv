Disclaimer​

This Guidebook was developed for all practitioners in preparing and implementing Financial Cooperation (FC) projects and provides them with assistance and examples of how to manage, monitor, and verify FC projects remotely.

The material and information provided in this Guidebook are, however, general in nature.
